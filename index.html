Honor 400 Pro Overview

The HONOR 400 Pro has a 6.7-inch 120Hz OLED display, powered by the Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 with 12GB RAM. It features a 200MP main camera, 50MP telephoto, 12MP ultra-wide, and a 50MP front camera. The phone includes a 6,000mAh battery with 100W wired and 50W wireless charging.

Honor Magic V5

The Honor Magic V5 is an ultra-slim foldable (8.8 mm folded, 4.21 mm open) with 120Hz dual OLED displays 6.43" outside and 7.95" inside. It runs on a Snapdragon 8 Elite with 16GB RAM, 512GB storage, and a 5,920 mAh battery (65W charging). Cameras include a 50MP main, 50MP ultrawide, 64MP telephoto, and dual 20MP selfies.
